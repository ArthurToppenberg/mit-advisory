

# MIT Advisory — Minimalistic Modern Website

## Design Direction
- **Light & clean** aesthetic with generous whitespace
- **Blue accent color** for CTAs, links, and highlights
- Modern sans-serif typography, professional tone
- Subtle animations on scroll (fade-in sections)
- Fully responsive (mobile, tablet, desktop)

## Pages & Structure

### 1. Homepage
- **Hero section**: Headline ("We help startups turn great ideas into scalable, profitable businesses") with a brief subtitle and a CTA button (e.g., "Get in touch")
- **What we specialize in**: 4 cards (Startup & SaaS, AI & Virtual Assistants, SAP Security, Industry Focus) with icons and short descriptions — each card links to its detail page
- **Brief about/trust section**: One-liner about the company with a link to the About page

### 2. Startup & SaaS Advisory
- Dedicated page with all the SaaS advisory content organized into clean sections: Product Definition, SaaS Models, MVP to Scale, AI as Advantage, Founder Sparring, and "The Result" summary

### 3. AI & Virtual Assistants
- Dedicated page covering AI Strategy, Virtual Assistants, Internal Productivity, AI-Enhanced Value Proposition, Responsible AI, and "The Result" summary

### 4. SAP Security & Control Systems
- Dedicated page with sections for Strategy & Governance, Role Design, GRC, PAM, IAM Integration, Data Protection, and "The Result" summary

### 5. Industry Focus
- Page covering AgTech & FoodTech and Manufacturing with their respective detail points

### 6. About / Team
- Company intro (founded 2023)
- Advisor profiles: Jens Toppenberg, Isabella Toppenberg, Gregor Rayman — each with photo placeholder, name, title, and bio

### 7. Contact
- Contact email, company address
- Simple contact form (name, email, message) — frontend only, no backend

## Navigation
- Clean top navbar with logo/brand name, page links, and a "Contact" CTA button
- Mobile: hamburger menu
- Footer with company info, nav links, and email
